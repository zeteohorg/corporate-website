zeteoh is pleased to announce its upcoming presentation at "Meetup Session," an innovative pitch event produced by Deloitte Tohmatsu Venture Support Co., Ltd. (hereinafter "Deloitte Tohmatsu Venture Support").

#### Event Overview

"Meetup Session," hosted by Deloitte Tohmatsu Venture Support, is an innovative pitch event designed specifically for seed to early-stage startups. The event reimagines the traditional pitch format by incorporating business mentoring elements, aiming to more effectively support the growth of participating companies.
This event is held regularly every Thursday at 6:00 PM, providing startup companies with valuable opportunities to receive feedback on their business plans and growth strategies from experienced professionals.

#### Presentation Details

- Date and Time: Thursday, January 30, 2025, from 18:00
- Location: Online Event
- Organizer: Deloitte Tohmatsu Venture Support Co., Ltd.

Future Prospects
Through participation in this event, zeteoh aims to refine its business strategy and create new business opportunities. We will continue to leverage the insights gained to further our business growth and enhance our value proposition.
