Our company has been selected as a member of the "Global50" by Station F, the world's largest startup incubation facility in Paris, France, and we delivered a pitch at an event held at the facility on May 22, 2024.

Global50 provides an excellent opportunity for Japanese companies and CVCs (Corporate Venture Capital) to meet European startups with cutting-edge innovations in AI, climate tech, deep tech, and more. This special event marks an important milestone in zeteoh's European expansion strategy.

[About Global50]
Global50 is an initiative that brings together the most promising innovators from around the world at Station F, the world's largest startup campus. It is known as a meetup that gathers startup stakeholders from France and Japan, hosted by Natural Capital at Station F to coincide with the 2024 Viva Technology event. Its purpose is to promote meaningful connections between Japanese companies and European startups developing breakthrough technologies.

[About Station F]
Based in Paris, France, Station F is one of the world's largest startup facilities, hosting over a thousand startups and numerous corporate partners including LVMH, BNP Paribas, Thales, Amazon, Meta, and Microsoft. The facility provides a comprehensive ecosystem for innovation, including investment funds, acceleration programs, fab labs, 3D printing services, and more.
