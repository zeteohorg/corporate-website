zeteoh, a provider of innovative solutions for the manufacturing sector, is proud to announce its selection into the prestigious Creative Destruction Lab (CDL) Paris program, hosted in partnership with HEC Paris.

CDL is a global, non-profit program dedicated to the commercialization and scaling of seed-stage, science-based companies. Over the course of the program, zeteoh will engage in an objective-based mentoring process guided by a carefully selected group of accomplished entrepreneurs, angel investors, economists, and scientists.

This selection validates the disruptive potential of our deep technology in driving efficiency and productivity improvements across the global manufacturing landscape. Our participation signifies that a world-class community recognizes our potential to create massive economic and societal value.

## Our Focus in CDL Paris

Through the CDL Paris experience, zeteoh will accelerate its efforts on the following key objectives:

**Commercialization of Technology**: Leverage practical insights from world-class mentors to translate our innovative science into a profitable and scalable business model.

**Investor Engagement**: Deepen relationships with seed-stage venture capital firms and angel investors who are focused on transformative deep tech in the industrial sector.

"Being selected for CDL Paris is a significant honor for our entire team. This opportunity for world-class mentorship is a critical step in realizing our vision of revolutionizing manufacturing. We invite manufacturers and investors to follow our journey of accelerated growth."

## About Creative Destruction Lab (CDL)

The Creative Destruction Lab is a non-profit network founded at the Rotman School of Management at the University of Toronto. It has generated billions in equity value by assisting science-based ventures to transition from science projects to highly scalable companies. CDL Paris is delivered in partnership with HEC Paris, a leading European business school.
