** Zeteoh Inc. is pleased to announce that our joint Proof of Concept (PoC) with Central Japan Railway Company was featured at the "2026 Technology Development Forum" in Nagoya on May 12-13th. The exhibit highlighted our achievements in precise location data acquisition within Shinkansen trains using Spatial AI. **

🔸 Key Achievements:

● ** Data Acquisition in Extreme Environments: ** We successfully tackled the challenge of acquiring precise indoor location data without fixed infrastructure in the harsh, high-vibration environment of a moving Shinkansen.

● ** High-Precision via AI Learning: ** Our proprietary AI models corrected initial sensor drift from over one train car length to a precision of within 3 meters in most scenarios.

● ** Operational Value: ** This precise data enables remote monitoring of crew locations during emergencies, demonstrating significant potential for enhancing railway safety and operational efficiency.


We will continue to accelerate industrial digital transformation by driving the development of 'Spatial AI' foundation models across every corner of the physical world.
